# Later Work

## Reusable BOMs from quote history

- Show a buyer's previous Xero quotes in the portal.
- Provide **Make into a BOM** on a quote and ask for the BOM name before creation.
- Copy the quote SKU and quantity lines into a portal-owned, editable BOM.
- Allow the buyer to add, remove, and adjust current catalogue products before adding the BOM to the cart.

Keep Xero quotes as accounting records and BOMs as reusable portal ordering templates. Do not make a saved BOM mutate the source Xero quote.
